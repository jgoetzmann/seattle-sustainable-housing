Zoning

Zoning is just one of many things that have exacerbated the housing crisis.

"Strict single-family zoning regulations limited housing supply, artificially raised prices, squandered the dream of homeownership for future generations, and blocked families from moving into neighborhoods with better schools and job opportunities, researchers and advocates say." - Meyersohn, CNN

Voting and pushing elected officials to change current zoning regulations is a crucial step in ending this crisis.
